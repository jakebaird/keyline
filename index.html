Why the Hinterland is Booming

With the Maroochydore CBD proceeding full steam ahead, alongside all the major infrastructure investment in the airport, light rail and highway, the Sunshine Coast is becoming an increasingly attractive destination for business and leisure.

This is great news for Nambour and the Hinterland. With stunning lifestyle options and a more relaxed way of life away from the bustle of the coast, more and more people are attracted to the feel and the affordability of the Hinterland. With current demand high, this is good news for homeowners as property values continue to slowly rise.
